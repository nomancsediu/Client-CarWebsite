Features & Innovations

Discover what makes the 2026 Camry stand out in its class.

Toyota Safety Sense 3.0

The 2026 Camry comes standard with Toyota Safety Sense 3.0 (TSS), featuring an upgraded Pre-Collision System, Lane Tracing Assist, Automatic High Beams, and the new Proactive Driving Assist that gently supports steering and braking for a safer, less fatiguing drive.

5th-Gen Hybrid Powertrain

Powered by Toyota's latest 5th-generation hybrid system, combining a 2.5L Dynamic Force engine with an electric motor. Delivers up to 225 hp (FWD) or 232 hp (AWD) with ~52 city / 49 highway mpg. A self-charging hybrid — no plug-in needed.

12.3" Multimedia Display

Features an upgraded 12.3-inch touchscreen with wireless CarPlay and Android Auto, over-the-air updates, and a 10-speaker JBL Premium Audio system. A head-up display projects speed and navigation directly onto the windshield.

Redesigned Exterior

The 2026 Camry debuts a sharper Hammerhead front fascia with sleeker LED headlights, a wider lower grille, and newly available two-tone paint options. The aerodynamic body reduces drag while 18-inch alloy wheels complete the bold new look.
